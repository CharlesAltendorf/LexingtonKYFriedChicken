About

Eating a double down from KFC.

Before starting college at the University of Kentucky, I didn't realize just how wild Lexington was for fried chicken. However, one simply cannot ignore the staggering popularity of fried chicken restaurants in the city. In putting together this study, I identified 31 locations representing 11 national chains as well as 3 local places. Lexington's population is about 330,000 people, meaning there is roughly 1 fried chicken joint for every 1,000 people. Click on the tiles above to find out more. Also feel free to contact me if you feel like I missed a spot.

Active Cincinnati

When I was nearing completion of MAP 671 course, I stumbled upon some interesting census information. In this map I looked at growing census tracts near private health clubs, I hope to explore other factors in the future.

2020 US Residency Map

View the Website

2020 data was collected using the American Community Survey API. Data was then copied to a csv file then joined into a geojson to be displayed using leaflet. Percentages were calculated by taking raw numbers listed and dividing the by the raw total residences in the data.

An Exemplary System

View the Article

During my time at Hardin County Water District No. 1, my manager requested a way to simply push out our data to several users without a cloud. This was for security reasons. I created a simple method to convert file geodatabases and symbols from Arcmap to QGIS. Users then simply had to plug into the server periodically to recieve updates. This system won us the KAMP Exemplary Systems Award in 2019 as well as got me pulished in the famous online GIS magazine, GISLounge.
